Introduction:

Mercedes is a DEX archer class part of the Heroes. Mercedes uses Dual Bowguns as primary weapons. As a secondary, Mercedes can equip Magic Arrows

For more information about Mercedes check out:

http://maplestory.nexon.net/game/classes-and-jobs/3157/mercedes

https://maplestory.fandom.com/wiki/Mercedes/Skills

Pros, Cons, and Concerns:

Pros:

  • Fun class with a linking / combo skill mechanic
  • Has innate HP and MP healing
  • Very mobile class with ability to quickly move up ladders and dash through map
  • A really good Link Skill that provides bonus EXP when hunting
  • Huge variety of attacks
  • Has a summon that attacks for you
  • You get to ride a unicorn into battle in 5th job!!!!

Cons:

  • Low HP but does not matter later on
  • You need to keep up a buff by linking skills, you may forget

Overall:

Very fun class especially with skills like Stunning Strikes, Leaf Tornado, Unicorn Spike, Ishtar's Ring, Spikes Royale and Wrath of Enlil, they are all satisfying skills to use! I also liked all the cool effects around my character from Water Shield and Elemental Knights. Overall felt very fun and satisfying to play
