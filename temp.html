Introduction:

Blaze Wizard is an INT mage class part of the Cygnus Knights. Blaze Wizards use Staves and Wands as primary weapons. As a secondary, Blaze Wizards can equip Jewels and Shields

A special thanks to TajMaJon for their help in making this overview!

For more information about Blaze Wizard check out:

http://maplestory.nexon.net/game/classes-and-jobs/2889/blaze-wizard

https://maplestory.fandom.com/wiki/Blaze_Wizard

How to create a Blaze Wizard:

To create one, choose the "Cygnus" option in the Character Creation screen and then continue through the story until you reach Lv. 10 and Ereve. You will see 5 NPCs on the left after talking to Neinheart. Talk to Oz to become a Blaze Wizard

Pros & Cons:

Pros:

  • Mage with double jump
  • Few buffs
    • Buffs have a long duration too (4 mins)
  • Cute summon that follows you and grants Magic Attack
  • Has a skill that regenerates MP
  • Some handy utilities for mobbing and bossing with skills such as:
    • Ignition
    • Flashfire
    • Cinder Maelstrom
    • Blazing Extinction
    • Burning Conduit
  • Has a revive
  • Cygnus Knight characters do not need Mastery Books

Cons:

  • Consumes a lot of MP ( A LOT!!)
  • Somewhat squishy around 4th Job
  • Main attack Orbital Flame is a bit clunky
    • For some reason, the damage it dealt felt really lackluster compared to how it looked
      • Especially using the Up Arrow attack
  • Lots of skills have cooldowns
  • Revive skill is a buff that has a duration and can expire

Overall:

The skills made me feel like they were going to blow things up but instead felt awkward especially with a skill like Dragon Blaze which only hits one enemy. Initially, it was fun but around 4th Job the class felt lacking. I am hoping 5th Job may fix that although I never got that far. Blaze Wizard was really a nice change of pace and offers a unique playstyle different from other mages especially with Orbital Flame

BEGINNER SKILLS:

  • Elemental Slash [Active Attack]: Performs a simple attack in front of you
  • Imperial Recall [Active]: Teleports you back to Ereve, Blaze Wizard's hometown
    • Has a cooldown (600 secs)
  • Elemental Harmony [Passive]: Increases INT per every 2 Levels
  • Elemental Expert [Passive]: Increases %Attack and %Magic Attack
  • Elemental Shift [Active]: Double jump skill
    • Can be assigned a Key Slot using or activated by pressing jump twice
      • While in the air, if you combine this with the Up Arrow Key, you will perform a vertical jump

Link Skill:

  • [Master Level 10] Cygnus Blessing [Passive]: Grants Attack and Magic Attack, Abnormal Status Resistance and %Elemental Resistance
    • This Link Skill can be stacked with other Cygnus Knights up to Level 10
      • This would be equivalent to 5 Lv. 120 Cygnus Knight characters

All Possible Boost Nodes obtainable:

V Skills:

  • Phalanx Charge [Active Summon]: Summons Spearmen of Cygnus Knights to charge forward damaging enemies that collide with it
    • The spearmen slow down upon touching an enemy
    • Pressing the Skill Key again will change their direction of movement
    • Unaffected by Damage Reflect
    • Has a cooldown (30 secs)

  • Empress Cygnus's Blessing [Buff]: Increases %Damage
    • Additionally periodically restores %Max HP and increases the %Damage buff (up to 90%)
    • Skill is enhanced after Lv. 245 becoming Transcendent Cygnus's Blessing
      • Damage buff will instead go up to 120% and additionally now reduces damage taken (including %Max HP attacks)
    • Has a cooldown (240 secs)

  • Mana Overload [Toggle Buff]: When toggled, increases the %Final Damage of all your skills (excluding summons)
    • Consumes %Max MP every second when toggled
      • Consumes %Max HP for classes without MP
    • Has a cooldown (59 - 30 secs depending on Skill Level)

  • Ethereal Form [Buff]: While active, you gain 100% Knockback Resistance and lose a fixed amount of MP instead of taking damage
    • When MP is 0 you take a fixed amount of HP damage
    • Has a cooldown (60 - 53 secs depending on Skill Level)

Class Specific V Skills:

  • Orbital Inferno [Active Attack]: Hurls out a giant fireball
    • Attack is unaffected by Damage Reflect and has additional %Ignore Def
    • Has a cooldown (5 secs)

  • Savage Flame [Active Attack]: Only usable when is active. The attack will differ depending on which animal was summoned
    • Enemies who are debuffed with the Ignition burn will grant 1 Flame Charge which is used to cast Savage Flame. A minimum of 2 Fire Charges is required to cast.
    • [Lion Attack]: Launches forward dealing massive damage and increases %Final Damage depending on remaining Fire Charges
    • [Fox Attack]: Creates fireballs that attack nearby enemies. The fireballs will regenerate and attack again depending on how many Fire Charges were used to cast
    • Has a cooldown (20 secs)

  • Inferno Sphere [Key-down Active Attack]: Deals damage when held down. The attack has additional %Crit Rate and %Ignore Def
    • This skill has a special resource called Phoenix Feathers which are prepared every 12 seconds and you can hold up to 15 feathers
    • This skill costs 1 feather every second to cast
    • Has a cooldown (2.5 secs)
